What are the best places to visit in and around Kashmir?
Jammu & Kashmir is home to numerous valleys like the Kashmir Valley, Sindh Valley, Chenab Valley
and Lidder Valley. Few important tourist attractions here are the Mughal Gardens, Srinagar, Pahalgam,
Gulmarg, Jammu and Patnitop.
Which is a better option to live in Kashmir - hotel or the houseboat?
Hotels in and around Kashmir are comfortable and luxurious. However, getting accommodation in a
houseboat can be dream come true and offer a unique experience, worth the investment and enjoy
unforgettable moments.
Which are the best places for shopping in Kashmir?
Srinagar boasts of having several local markets that can be visited to shop. The popular markets here
are Lal Chowk, Polo View, Badshah Chowk, Residency Road, Floating Market, Kashmiri Government
Arts Emporium, and Zaina Khandal Road.
