Day 5: PAHLAGAM - SRINAGAR - 23rd, Nov 2021
Night stay: Srinagar Houseboat
After breakfast the day is free to explore Lidder valley at your own or you can pick one of the below
given options by taking a local cab at your own cost. Option 1. Visit to Aru Valley which is a small
village, 15 km upstream the Lidder River. The route is picturesque with ample campsites. This
village is the starting point for treks to the Kolahoi glacier. Option 2. Visit to Chandanwari, 16 Km
which is the starting point of sacred Amarnath Yatra. Chandanwari is famous for snow sledging on
a snow bridge. En-route you can visit Hajan, an idyllic picnic spot and Betaab valley. in evenig
hours return to srinagar , enjoy Boat Ride on Dal Lake by a gondola Boat called ”SHIKARA” witness
the ride on lake, called Venice of India & experience the aquatic life of Srinagar evening return back
to Houseboat for Dinner & overnight Stay ..
